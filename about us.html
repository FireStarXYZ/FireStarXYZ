About Us

Here at SMG Badminton, we're dedicated to giving players the best opportunities and resources they need to improve and perform at their best.

What we offer:

Our sessions run on three days: Tuesday, Wednesdays and Thursdays. Our sessions include 1-to-1 coaching, small group sessions and matchplay sessions as well.
