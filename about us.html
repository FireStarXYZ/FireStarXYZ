About Us

Here at SMG Badminton, we're dedicated to giving players the absolute best opportunities and resources they need to improve and perform at their peak.

What we offer:

Our sessions run on three days: Tuesday, Wednesdays and Thursdays. Our sessions include 1-to-1 coaching, small group sessions and matchplay sessions as well.

Make sure to contact us for more details about sessions if you're interested!

Who is this for?

We ask that players have a passion for playing badminton and improving their game. We would also like them to be of an intermediate standard when starting at SMG.
