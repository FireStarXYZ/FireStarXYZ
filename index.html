Welcome to SMG Performance Badminton!

At SMG Performance Badminton, we’re committed to helping aspiring players reach their full potential through structured, high-quality training options tailored to different needs and goals.

Ready to take your game to the next level?

Visit our Enquiries Page to get started—we’d love to hear from you and help plan the right training path for you.

Our sessions run on three days: Tuesday, Wednesdays and Thursdays. Our sessions include 1-to-1 coaching, small group sessions and matchplay sessions as well.

Why join us:

Competitive play - we make sure all our sessions offer the highest level of competition and challenge to all, we want experienced players who want to always aim for the best

Sense of community - all of our players are driven, successful and most importantly passionate for badminton, at SMG we always try to foster a deep sense of teamwork between all.
